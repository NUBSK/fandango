About

Fandango was a project that was voted as a winning solution on CulTIP Challenges 2014, sponsored by British Council Macedonia. The idea was submitted by National and University Library "St. Clement of Ohrid" - Macedonia in partnership with MASIT. The final product was developed with support from Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering (FINKI).
